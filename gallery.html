This engaging geometric painting features a vibrant display of overlapping petals in primary colors—red, yellow, and blue—arranged in a dynamic pattern that gives the illusion of constant movement. At the core of the composition, the interplay of these petals creates a kaleidoscopic effect, as if they are perpetually spinning and intertwining. Adding a unique perspective to the artwork, four blue half-moon shapes are strategically positioned at each corner, resembling the edges of a looking glass. This clever framing invites the viewer to feel as though they are peering through a lens, focusing on the colorful dance at the center. The overall effect is both captivating and visually striking, drawing the observer into a deeper exploration of shape and color dynamics.

This geometric painting features a striking dodecahedron at its center, serving as a vivid exploration of color and form. The three front-facing faces of the dodecahedron are painted in bold primary colors: red, yellow, and blue. At each point where these colors meet, the edges blend into the corresponding secondary colors—orange, green, and purple—demonstrating a seamless transition and the harmonious interplay of hues. Surrounding the dodecahedron, the composition extends outward with shapes that complement the central figure, enhancing the sense of unity and coherence. This thoughtful arrangement not only highlights the relationships between primary and secondary colors but also creates a visually cohesive and balanced artwork, inviting viewers to appreciate the unity within the diversity of shapes and colors.

This painting features interlacing shapes that resemble parachutes, arranged in dynamic opposition as if inflating each other. The composition is divided into three sections, each emphasized by a pair of colors: red and orange represent the dynamic interplay between fiery energy and soothing warmth; blue and green symbolize the harmony between the expansiveness of the sky and the life-giving essence of nature; yellow and purple contrast brightness with depth, invoking a sense of illumination and mystery. This arrangement of colors and forms creates a visual flow of billowing shapes across the canvas, offering a compelling exploration of contrast and harmony.

This arrangement forms a complex and harmonious mosaic when the six primary and secondary colors—yellow, blue, red, purple, green, and orange—are applied. Each color is strategically placed to enhance the overall symmetry and visual impact of the painting, where the interplay of shapes and colors illustrates a perfect balance, embodying both mathematical precision and artistic expression.

This geometric painting ingeniously uses circles arranged in a specific matter to facilitate the drawing of square view forms, featuring two golden ratio squares that anchor the composition. The precise placement of circles not only guides the construction of these squares but also accentuates their proportion and balance, creating a visually harmonious composition. This technique highlights the intersection of mathematical precision and artistic expression, making the painting both a visual delight and an educational piece. In this geometric painting, six overlapping circles form a complex and mesmerizing design. Each circle is uniquely split into two colors, creating a vibrant and symbolic interplay. The yellow and purple circles are prominent, with yellow symbolizing the spirit and purple representing beings. These circles are strategically placed both in the forefront and in the back, serving as an anchor for the composition. The blue and green circle merges the essence of heaven and angels, floating gently within the arrangement. Lastly, the red and orange circle intertwines the realms of hell and demons, adding a fiery contrast to the cooler tones. The interlocking circles and the dual-toned design generate a dynamic balance, evoking a sense of spiritual and existential depth.
